Scarcity in the Digital Realm

The Digital Gold Institute is a research and development center about scarcity in the digital realm (bitcoin and crypto-assets) and blockchain technology.
Focused on its fellowship program, the Institute promotes the digital scarcity experiment in the public debate and the scientific academia through R&D, teaching, consulting, and advising.
We explore the foundations and can assist in understanding them: cryptography, monetary theory, computer networking, game theory.
Of course, we write code and love to get our hands dirty in programming and technology.
